Experimental Research

Project 1: Effects of different physiological levels of estradiol on the chemosensitivity of HR+ breast cancer

• Aim: To explore the effects of different physiological levels of estradiol (E2) on the chemosensitivity of breast cancer to epirubicin (EPI).

• Methods: CCK-8 assay was used to observe the proliferation of MCF-7 and MDA-MB-231 cells treated with E2 (0, 50, 500, 2500, 5000, 10000pg/ml) for 24h．After pretreatment with different doses of E2 for 12h and then treated with EPI alone for 24h, the effects of E2 on the chemosensitivity of MCF-7 and MDA-MB-231 cells to EPI were detected by CCK-8 assay. MCF-7 tumor-bearing nude mice were randomly assigned to Non-OVX+EPI group (group B), OVX+EPI group (group D), OVX+E2+EPI group (group E, F and G) and control group. After being treated with EPI, tumor inhibition rates were calculated to detect the effects of E2 on tumor inhibition of EPI chemotherapy.

•Conclusion: E2 dose-dependently promoted the chemosensitivity of MCF-7 cells to EPI, suggested that applied the chemotherapy in physiological periods with higher E2 level might improve the chemotherapy efficacy in HR+ breast cancer.

Project 2: Effect of dexamethasone during chemotherapy of breast cancer

• Aim: To investigate the effect of dexamethasone (DEX) on the anticancer activity of anthracycline chemotherapy in routine TAC chemotherapy regimen of breast cancer.

• Methods: The effects of different concentrations of dexamethasone combined with doxorubicin on the viability of breast cancer cells were detected by CCK-8. Flow cytometry was used to detect the effect of dexamethasone and doxorubicin on breast cancer cells apoptosis rate; real-time quantitative PCR and western blot were used to detect the effects of different concentrations of dexamethasone and doxorubicin on the expression of bcl-2 family related genes and protein.

• Conclusion:The conventional dexamethasone dosage in TAC chemotherapy regimen may reduce the effect of doxorubicin chemotherapy by inhibiting the apoptosis pathway of bcl-2, promote the proliferation activity of breast cancer cells in vitro and inhibit the apoptosis of cells. Therefore, TAC chemotherapy should be avoided using dexamethasone.

• MajorResponsibility: 1. Retrieve and review relevant literatures 2. Responsible for the operation of some experiments, such as cell culture, western blot, flow cytometry and Hoechst staining etc. 3. Discussion of the results and conclusion.
